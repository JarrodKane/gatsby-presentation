<Layout title="GraphQL">

- Not organised in endpoints, but rather in terms and types, it easier to visualise and understand the connections
- Can get more than one resource at once, gets all your data in one request
- Can collect your data from many different forms at build time, and compile it into the one GrpahQL api
- Always return predictable results
- Fast, stable
